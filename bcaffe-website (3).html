Our story

A neighborhood café, brewed with heart

دەربارەی مە — چیرۆکا مە

B Caffe began with a simple idea: bring the coffee our grandparents loved — Kezwan, Turkish, Arabic — together with the drinks a new generation craves, all under one warm roof in Zakho. Every cup is brewed to order, every sweet made fresh, and every guest greeted like family.

Craft in every cup

Beans dialed in daily, milk steamed to silk, and traditional coffees brewed slow — the way they deserve.

Rooted in Kurdistan

From Kezwan to Sahlab, we keep local flavors alive — served in both Kurdish and English, for everyone.

A place to gather

Honest prices, generous hospitality, and a space made for long talks and short breaks alike.

Kezwan (قەهوە كزوان) is a caffeine-free Kurdish coffee brewed from roasted wild pistachio (terebinth) seeds. It's earthy, slightly nutty, and one of our proudest traditions — just 2,000 IQD a cup.
